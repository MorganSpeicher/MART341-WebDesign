My Guidlines

When I cook I like to follow a few healthy habits:

  • High Protein
  • Low Calorie
  • Organic/Better For You Foods
  • Delicious!

A lot of the time I find that I'm missing core ingredients, usually I substitute or just leave it out entirely.
Usually it turns out really good! I've found that you don't always need the expensive odd-end ingrients to make something yummy.
Simple meals can be cheap and easy to make too.
